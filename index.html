Naumi Auckland Airport offers luxury accommodation with stylish rooms, surrounded by quiet gardens. A fitness centre and Asian-influenced restaurant are featured.

The property is designed with contemporary yet bold accents, inspired by New Zealand's native Tui bird. The spacious nests (rooms) at the property boast private patios and each room is equipped with a Smart flat-screen TV with 60 satellite channels and a Netflix channel. Free unlimited WiFi across multiple devices is also featured in the rooms.

The front desk can help guests with travel arrangements.

The on-site restaurant features an extensive à la carte menu, featuring Asian fusion cuisine. The bar offers signature cocktails, house beer and a range of snacks. For breakfast included rates, breakfast is only included for adults. Children are charged NZD 15.
